# Inspection plots

This folder is generated by `export_muse_221e_dataset.m` when `cfg.write_inspection_plots = true`.

The plots are intended only for sanity checking the dataset after 221e compensation, SLERP resampling, onset alignment, and robot-to-MUSE frame transformation. They are not required for AI training.

## Per-trial plots

Path:

```text
Inspection/trajectories/<trajectory>/<axis>/
```

Files:

- `00_alignment_onset_detection.png`: pre-alignment onset-detection diagnostics. It shows the MUSE gyroscope-norm threshold and the ROBOT quaternion-difference threshold, with onset and crop-start markers.
- `01_quaternion_overlay.png`: MUSE and robot quaternion components after alignment and frame compensation. The two curves should have comparable morphology if the export is coherent.
- `02_error_norm_angle.png`: Euler-error components, Euler-error norm, SO(3) angular error, and error-vector direction angles.
- `03_component_rms_contribution.png`: RMS contribution of roll, pitch, and yaw error.
- `04_alignment_activity_overlay.png`: normalized MUSE and ROBOT activity signals before and after crop/alignment. This is intended to verify that the movement onsets are brought to the same aligned time base.
- `inspection_summary.txt`: compact numerical diagnostics for the same trajectory-axis pair.

## Global plots

Path:

```text
Inspection/summary/
```

Files:

- `01_global_error_by_axis.png`: mean RMS Euler-error norm by axis, plus P95 Euler norm and RMS SO(3) angle.
- `02_trial_rms_distribution.png`: per-trial RMS Euler-error norm distribution, grouped by axis.
- `03_dominant_component_counts.png`: dominant Euler-error component counts.
- `04_export_status_by_axis.png`: valid and failed/skipped exports by axis.
- `inspection_global_summary.txt`: compact numerical summary of the global inspection.

## Interpretation

These figures should be used to detect gross problems such as wrong onset detection, missing alignment, wrong axis folder matching, excessive quaternion discontinuities, or a single axis systematically producing much larger errors than the others.
They are not statistical validation plots and should not replace the quantitative CSV/MAT outputs.
